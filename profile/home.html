Programming is fun?

Turns out I genuinely enjoy the programming. I seem to be part of the rare few who finds the reward of discovering a solution, worth going through the struggle to find. All of those long nights figuring out how pointers working and even programming classic problems such as Wumpus World were well worth the reward.
